Poplar Studio Work
Some of the work produced during my three years at Poplar Studio (2019-).
Shopify App
Work Project

The Shopify app is born as a partner app to Poplar Studio’s own CMS, which allows Shopify retailers to embed AR technology into their Shopify stores within the Shopify environment.

See this project
Poplar CMS
Work Project

The Poplar CMS is born as a Content Management System that allows clients to manage their 3D files and the relationships these maintain with their product catalogue.

See this project
Head Model Positioning Tool
Work Project

As part of the CMS, we have developed two tools that give our clients control over how a 3D model will look (and fit a human head) in our Embed Unit.

See this project
3D Configurator
Work Project

The 3D Configurator is an exploratory project to look into ways to enable users of the Embed Unit to customise a product.

See this project
Embed Unit
Work Project

The Embed Unit is (in my opinion) one of Poplar Studio’s most interesting products. Designed and built completely in-house, it enables consumers to view products from all angles, try them out in their own environment and even try them on themselves.
